Our Services

Comprehensive Support for Glasgow Families

Family Support

This service is tailored to provide trauma-informed bespoke guidance and relationship-based assistance for families when they need it most. We're here to lend a hand, promote strong bonds, navigate challenges and offer a supportive network for parents and caregivers.

Respite Sitting

Community based respite where children 16 years and under are provided with engaging experiences and quality time outside their homes fostering positive interactions and personal growth.

  • Weekly/fortnightly sessions for up to 8 weeks
  • 4 hour sessions
  • Same respite provider for consistency
  • Individualised care plans

Activities include exploring local parks, museums and creative/sensory activities within our accessible Community Flat.

Respite Care

This service provides children and young people with a safe and nurturing, home away from home experience, giving parents and carers a well-deserved extended break.

  • Weekly/fortnightly sessions for up to 8 weeks
  • Up to 18hrs respite provided
  • Same respite provider for consistency
  • Individualised care plans

Geeza Chance

A program for children and young people in kinship care aged 8-16 years, providing activities and workshops to build confidence, social and life skills. Through outings, skill-based sessions, and peer support, it creates a nurturing, inclusive space for children and young people to thrive.
