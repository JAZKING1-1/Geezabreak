Volunteer Stories

Sarah's Journey

Sarah started volunteering with us 3 years ago as a play session helper. Today, she's one of our most valued team members, coordinating volunteer activities and mentoring new volunteers.

"Volunteering with Geeza Break has given me so much more than I could ever give. The children, the families, and the amazing team have become my extended family."

Mike's Impact

Mike brings his IT skills to help us maintain our website and develop new digital tools. His technical expertise has helped us reach more families and streamline our operations.

"I wanted to use my skills for something meaningful. Geeza Break gave me that opportunity and showed me how technology can make a real difference in people's lives."

Emma's Passion

Emma volunteers as a respite carer, providing overnight support for families who need a break. Her compassionate nature and dedication make her an invaluable part of our team.

"Being able to give families the breathing space they need is incredibly rewarding. Seeing the difference it makes in their lives keeps me coming back."

James's Commitment

James helps with our fundraising events and community outreach. His enthusiasm and organizational skills have helped us raise thousands for our services.

"Every pound raised means more support for families. It's amazing to see how our community comes together to help those who need it most."

Volunteer Roles Available

Play Session Helper

Support our play sessions by helping children with activities, games, and crafts. No special skills required - just enthusiasm and patience!

2-4 hours/week

Respite Carer

Provide overnight or weekend respite care for families. Help with bedtime routines, morning care, and light household tasks.

Flexible hours

Admin Support

Help with administrative tasks like data entry, filing, organizing events, and maintaining our volunteer database.

4-8 hours/week

Fundraising Coordinator

Organize fundraising events, manage social media campaigns, and help us reach more people in our community.

6-10 hours/week

Maintenance Helper

Help with basic maintenance tasks around our facilities, gardening, and keeping our play areas safe and clean.

Flexible hours

Mentor

Mentor new volunteers, share your experience, and help build our volunteer community through training and support.

2-6 hours/week

Student Placements

We welcome students from various disciplines who are looking to gain valuable experience in child care, community work, and charity management. Our placements offer hands-on learning opportunities and the chance to make a real difference.

Learning Experience

Gain practical experience in child care, community engagement, and charity operations while earning academic credits.

Professional Development

Develop transferable skills in communication, teamwork, and project management that will benefit your future career.

Making a Difference

Contribute to meaningful work that directly improves the lives of children and families in our community.

Networking

Build connections with professionals in the charity sector and expand your professional network.

Suitable for students in: Social Work, Psychology, Education, Business, IT, Health & Social Care, and other related fields.

Placement Duration: From 1 week work experience to 12-month placements depending on your course requirements.
