Geeza Break Community Flat

The Geeza Break Community Flat is a welcoming and fully equipped space, designed to support families by offering a safe, homely environment. It includes a kitchen and a toy room, making it perfect for respite care, cooking, arts & crafts, board games, and family support services.

🎥 Sensory Room Tour

✨ How the Community Flat is Used

  • Cooking sessions – learn, cook, and share meals together.
  • Arts & Crafts – creative activities for children and families.
  • Board Games – fun, interactive sessions that encourage bonding.
  • Family Support Services – safe and supportive environment for families.
  • Contact Visits – a nurturing space for children in care or those being reunited with family.

🌈 The Sensory Room

Our Community Flat features a dedicated Sensory Room that supports the health and well-being of both children and parents. This calming and interactive space provides opportunities for comfort, play, and personal growth.

🧘 De-stress

Helps calm overstimulated minds, providing comfort and refocusing attention.

👀 Sensory Stimulation

Encourages exploration and supports understanding of the world through senses.

🎨 Learning Through Play

Montessori-inspired activities build problem-solving, creativity, and brain connections.

⚖️ Balance & Movement

Improves motor skills, coordination, and spatial awareness.

🗣️ Communication Skills

Encourages verbal and non-verbal interaction, especially supportive for children with autism.

🤝 Socialisation

Provides a safe and calm environment for building relationships and empathy.
