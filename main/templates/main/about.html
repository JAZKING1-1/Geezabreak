About Geeza Break

30+ Years of Supporting Glasgow Families

Our Story

Founded in 1992, Geeza Break emerged from a community need in Glasgow's East End. What started as a grassroots initiative has grown into a vital support system for hundreds of families across Glasgow.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support services that strengthen families, enhance child development, and build resilient communities across Glasgow.

Our Values

  • Respect for every family's unique journey
  • Commitment to excellence in service delivery
  • Integrity in all our interactions
  • Innovation in addressing community needs
  • Inclusivity and accessibility for all
